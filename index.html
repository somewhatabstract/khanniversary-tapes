Outro · liner notes

Thank you to Dave, Matthew, Walt, and Eric for lending their ears and their taste to these tapes — and to everyone at Khan Academy for nine years of meaningful work alongside genuinely wonderful people. It's a rare gift to have somewhere worth showing up to, week after week, and this is my small thank-you for it.

I've been writing songs for a little over thirty years now, and the biggest thing all that time has taught me is that the making matters more than the outcome. So whatever your thing is — songs, sketches, bread, birdhouses, one-take demos (which none of these songs were, by the way — so many takes), perfectly prepared pedagogical problems — make it, and when you're feeling brave, share it. Somebody out there needs exactly the thing that only you could make.
